How Sharing Works
1️⃣ You create a Khatm
The app creates a special link that contains your entire Khatm.
2️⃣ Share on WhatsApp
Tap the green Share on WhatsApp button. The link goes straight to your family group.
3️⃣ Family claims paras
Anyone who opens the link sees the Khatm, enters their name, and claims their para.
4️⃣ Share updates back
After claiming or completing, they tap Share Update to send the updated Khatm back to the group.
✅ No sign-in. No database. No banking details.
Everything is in the link. It works like passing a piece of paper around — except on WhatsApp.
